How To

This page is to teach you how to use the bot.
Goal
The goal of using this discord bot is to splat Salmonoids, as you message in the server salmons can spawn randomly. By splatting them you will receive rewards
Splatting
Once a salmon spawns you can say !splat [salmon_name], depending on the health of the salmon you may need to splat it multiple times.
It is possible for you to miss, this has no downside, so simply attempt again!
Power Eggs (Points)
Power eggs, also known as points is how score is kept between players. Currently there is no use for them, and is solely to show off how many salmonoids you have splatted.
Boss Salmon
Occasionally a Boss Salmon can spawn, when they do they have significantly more health, and can require specific items to beat (not yet implemented)
They also will have a chance to give you golden eggs, using golden eggs you can purchase items in the shop
Store
By saying !shop you can view the store run by marigold, also known as the staff. One of the most notable items you can purchase is the specials.
Specials
Specials have many benefits, they can allow you to summon salmon, double your damage, one shot the salmon, and many more abilities!
Salmonmeter
The Salmonmeter works slightly differently in this bot compared to the game, however it serves the same purpose. In this bot the meter is based on how many salmon have been splatted within the server, and once the meter hits 100%, a king salmon will spawn
King Salmon
King salmon are extremely rare, requiring for the salmon meter to be full before they can spawn. Once they do they will require a significant amount of damage in order to splat them
Once splatted they will drop scales, these are distributed between all players that participated. The more damage the user has done the more likely it is for them to receive a scale.
Scales
Once you splat a king salmon you get scales, currently these have no use, but are planed to in the future.
